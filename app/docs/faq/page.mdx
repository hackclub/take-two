# FAQ

## Who can participate?

Any Hack Club member with a Slack account can participate in Take Two.

## How much funding can I get?

Up to **$75** for replacement parts if your first build attempt didn't work out.

## What counts as a hardware project?

Anything physical that you've built — robots, PCBs, IoT devices, 3D-printed gadgets, custom keyboards, etc. Software-only projects don't qualify.

## What if my project already works?

Great! Submit it through the [prebuilt form](https://forms.hackclub.com/take-two-prebuilt) to get verified hardware hacker status.

## How long does review take?

Reviews typically happen within a few days. You can check your project status on your dashboard.

## Can I submit multiple projects?

Yes! Each project goes through its own review process.

## What is verified hardware hacker status?

It's a badge that shows you've successfully completed a hardware project. Keep an eye out — it'll unlock things in the future.
