# Getting Started

Take Two is a Hack Club YSWS that gives you a second chance at your hardware projects. Whether your first attempt didn't quite work out or you want to level up an existing build, we've got you covered.

## How it works

1. **Build your project** — attempt your hardware build and document your journey in a `BLOG.md` file
2. **Need changes?** — if things didn't go as planned, get up to **$75** for new parts
3. **Verified status** — if your project works, earn verified hardware hacker status (this will be used later 👀)

## Getting started

1. Sign in with your Hack Club Slack account using the button in the top right
2. Fill out the submission form at [forms.hackclub.com/take-two](https://forms.hackclub.com/take-two)
3. Check your dashboard to track your project status

Already have a working hardware project? Use the [prebuilt form](https://forms.hackclub.com/take-two-prebuilt) instead.
