# Submitting Your Project

## What you need

- A GitHub repo with your project code
- A `BLOG.md` file documenting your build process
- Photos or a demo video of your project

## The BLOG.md file

Your `BLOG.md` should cover:

- **What you built** — a brief description of your project
- **What went wrong** — the problems you ran into
- **What you learned** — key takeaways from the experience
- **What's next** — if you're requesting a second take, what you'd do differently

## Submitting

1. Make sure your repo is public on GitHub
2. Fill out the [submission form](https://forms.hackclub.com/take-two)
3. Your project will appear on your dashboard once it's been received
4. A reviewer will check your submission and update its status

## After submission

You can track your project status on your **dashboard**. Projects go through these stages:

- **Submitted** — we've received your project
- **In review** — a reviewer is looking at your submission
- **Approved** — your project has been approved for parts funding
- **Built & verified** — your project works and you've earned verified status
