# Rewards!

Here's the reward system! At the end of every season you'll get the rewards you unlock shipped to you. Dashboard will be updated to include your currently unlocked rewards soon!

## Lifetime rewards:

### Ranks:

#### Verified hardware hacker

Build at least 1 hardware project with a working demo

- Verified hardware hacker rank on profile
- Collectible pin unlocked
- Eligible for expert status in summer hardware program

#### Celebrity:

Get at least 500 upvotes on reddit, OR a Hackaday/Hackerster.io article!

- Celebrity rank on profile
- Collectible pin unlocked
- More coming!

### Powerups:

#### Undercity orpheus pico

Build one of your Highway projects, get an Undercity Orpheus pico:

![undercity pico image](https://cdn.hackclub.com/019cf84b-074d-7a7b-8a54-4c7cc81712b4/orphpico-compressed-colors.png)

#### Hackpad airBNB relic

Build a Hackpad from v1 or v2, get an original leftover PCB from the infamous Hackpad airBNB

## Season rewards

### Season 1:

#### Season 1 competitor:

Build at least 1 hardware project during season 1!

- Season 1 competitor rank on profile
- Season 1 competitor pin

#### Season 1 finalist:

- Finish top 3 on leaderboard
- Season 1 finalist rank on profile
- $150 travel stipend to go to [Rocky Mountain RepRap Festival 2026](https://rockymountainreprapfestival.com/) from April 17-18
    - _if you can't go, defaults to next available person_
