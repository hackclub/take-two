# Overview

Welcome to Take Two! Get a second take on your hardware project.

Here's how it works:

1. Build your project!
2. Afterwards, write a BLOG.md file reflecting on your experience / journey building your project. Link it in your dashboard!
3. Submit an update to your project in the [submission form](https://forms.hackclub.com/take-two)

If your project worked, collect [rewards](/docs/rewards). If it didn't, get up to $75 to rebuild it! You should include what needs to be fixed in your blog.

### I have a project I already built and it works!

You can skip the BLOG.md step - just submit the form!

In the future there'll be perks to writing a BLOG.md file regardless though, so I would recommend writing one if you have time!

### My project isn't showing up!

Make sure you have your emails linked under your profile! You may have submitted a project with a different email.
