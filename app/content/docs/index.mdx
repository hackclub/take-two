# Overview

Welcome to Hardware! Get a second take on your hardware project.

Here's how it works:

### 1. Start building your project!
**Any project** listed under your dashboard is eligible for Hardware!

Get that old pile of parts that you told yourself you were going to build 6 months ago and START BUILDING!! You only have until the season ends up

*missing projects? try adding other emails under your [dashboard](/dashboard)*

### 2. Document your build process + results!

Take lots of pictures while building! Write some jot-notes about what your experience was like!

By the end of it, you should have:
- A short demo video! (preferably on youtube or something similar)
- Pictures of your project IN REAL LIFE!!!
- A list of notes from your journey building your project 

Write a short BLOG.md file reflecting on your experiences! *this is optional if your project works, but if you need a grant to repair your project it is mandatory*

### 3. Update your project's card on the dashboard

This step is super important! After getting all of the new data, you should update your project's info's in the card

<img src="https://cdn.hackclub.com/019d225a-492a-72c0-a935-7b366be001e0/image.png" className="max-h-96" />

Make sure you have
- An image of your project in real life
- A short video with the current state
- A BLOG.md, if you need a grant!

### 4. Submit & collect rewards!

Submit your project to the [submission form](https://forms.hackclub.com/take-two)!

If your project fully works, collect [rewards](/docs/rewards).

 If it didn't, get up to $75 to rebuild it! You should include what needs to be fixed in your blog.

Any quesitons? You should head on over to the [Frequently Asked Questions](/docs/faq) page!
